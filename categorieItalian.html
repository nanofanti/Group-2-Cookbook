Pasta La Vista: Unveiling the Comedy in Italian Cuisine

Italian cuisine has been around since before Ancient Roman times. It was heavily influenced by other Mediterranean cultures during its early days, particularly the Greeks and Jews. Still, it developed its unique style over time that combines simplicity with sophistication. Traditional dishes such as lasagna, ravioli, risotto, and spaghetti are based on these ancient recipes but have evolved significantly to include many different ingredients and techniques. What comes to mind when you think of Italian food? Chances are it's pizza!
