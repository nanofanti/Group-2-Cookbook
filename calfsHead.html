"Calf's Head"

Difficulty: ⏺ ⏺ ⭐️ ⭐️ ⭐️

Time: 2 Hours

Disgustingness: ⏺ 🤢 🤢 🤢 🤢

Ingredients

  • 1 Calf's Head 🐮
  • 1kg Carrot 🥕
  • 1 Onion 🧅
  • 2 Garlic Cloves 🧄
  • Wine 🍷
  • Vinegar 🍶
  • Bouquet Garni (parsley, thyme, bay leaf) 🌱
  • 2 Tbsp Flour 🍚
  • 1 Tbso Coarse Salt 🧂
  • 1 Tbsp Pepper 🧂

Instructions

  • Start by peeling the carrots and slicing it into pieces that are neither too thin nor too thick.
  • Peel the onion and finely chop it into rings.
  • Finally, peel and crush garlic cloves.
  • Place the boneless and rolled calf's head in a casserole dish, then cover it completely with water.
  • Add carrots, onion, garlic, vinegar and bouquet garni.
  • Sprinkle it with coarse salt and pepper to your liking.
  • Mix the flour in a little water and add to the casserole dish.
  • Bring it to the boil, cover, lower the heat and simmer for about 2 hours on low heat.
